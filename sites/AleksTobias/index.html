Also known as 21, Blackjack is a card game where players aim to have a hand value closest to 21 without exceeding it. Each card has a point value, and players decide whether to "hit" for additional cards or "stand" to compete against the dealer. Strategy and probability come into play, making Blackjack a popular choice for casino enthusiasts.

Slots, or slot machines, are iconic casino games that have transitioned seamlessly into the digital age. Players spin reels adorned with various symbols, aiming to align matching symbols on paylines for a win. The allure of themed graphics, bonus features, and the potential for substantial jackpots make slots a widely enjoyed and accessible form of gambling entertainment.

Baccarat is a classic casino card game known for its simplicity and elegance. Players bet on the outcome of the game, choosing between the "Player," "Banker," or a tie. The objective is to have a hand closest to nine points. With minimal decision-making involved, Baccarat is a game of chance where luck plays a crucial role.

Poker is a popular card game that combines skill, strategy, and an element of chance. Players compete to create the best possible hand of cards, using a combination of community cards and their own hidden cards. Variants like Texas Hold'em and Omaha involve betting rounds, bluffing, and carefully reading opponents to achieve success.
