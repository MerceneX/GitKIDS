Top 10 Chinese Tire Brands

1. Michelin (China)

Though Michelin is a French brand, its significant operations in China have made it a prominent player in the local market.

2. Zhongce Rubber Group

Zhongce is one of the largest tire manufacturers in China, known for its wide range of products including passenger and truck tires.

3. Giti Tire

Giti Tire is known for its innovation and quality, producing tires for passenger cars, trucks, and buses.

4. Linglong Tire

Linglong has a strong presence both in China and internationally, offering a variety of tires for different vehicles.

5. Doublestar Tire

Doublestar is recognized for its high-performance tires and strong export market.

6. Triangle Tire

Triangle specializes in producing tires for passenger cars, trucks, and specialty vehicles.

7. Sailun Tire

Sailun is known for its cost-effective tires and has gained a reputation for quality among budget-conscious consumers.

8. Haida Tire

Haida focuses on producing high-quality tires and has a growing presence in the global market.

9. Goodride Tire

Goodride is known for offering affordable tire options without compromising on quality.

10. ChaoYang Tire

ChaoYang specializes in tires for motorcycles, bicycles, and other specialty vehicles.
