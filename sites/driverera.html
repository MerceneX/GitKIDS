The Driver Era

Welcome to Driver Era

The Driver Era is an American musical duo formed by brothers Ross and Rocky Lynch. They are known for their diverse musical style, combining elements of alternative, indie, and electronic music.

About Driver Era

The band originated from the pop-rock band R5, with Ross Lynch as the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist and Rocky Lynch as the lead guitarist. The Driver Era was officially formed in 2018, allowing the brothers to explore a more mature and experimental sound.
