Marko Gluhak

Marko Gluhak, a legendary figure in motorcycling, never takes off his red and black business suit and helmet, creating a mysterious yet iconic image. With a Moto Guzzi V85 TT Nero Etna, he embarks on audacious adventures, from the winding Dolomites to deserts and coastal highways. His suit signifies a unique blend of adventure and the corporate world, a symbol of a life lived on the edge and the spirit of exploration. Marko has become an emblem of courage and a reminder that great journeys await those who embrace the unknown.
