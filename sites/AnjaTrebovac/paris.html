Explore Paris

Paris, the capital city of France, is one of the most iconic cities in the world. Known for its beautiful landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral, it is a top destination for travelers around the world.

Must-See Landmarks

Some of the top places to visit in Paris include:

  • The Eiffel Tower
  • The Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • The Champs-Élysées and Arc de Triomphe
  • Sacré-Cœur and Montmartre

Paris is not only a city of history, but also a vibrant hub for art, fashion, and cuisine. Every corner of the city offers something unique to discover.
